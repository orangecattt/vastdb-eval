The vulnerability arises because when `node->v` points to the local `node->sorted` member and `nodeDestruct` is called, it frees `node->sorted.val.l.vals`, but later if `stack` is reallocated during a child's processing, the original `stack` memory moves, making the stale `node` pointer in the loop refer to freed or reallocated memory, where the condition `tr_variantIsContainer(node->v)` accesses the dangling pointer to `node->sorted`.
