The vulnerability occurs because the code assigns `pbkdf2_salt` from `pbkdf2_param->salt->value.octet_string` without verifying that the `salt` ASN1_TYPE pointer is non-NULL or that its `type` field corresponds to `V_ASN1_OCTET_STRING`, allowing a NULL pointer to be assigned to `pbkdf2_salt`. This NULL pointer is subsequently dereferenced at `pbkdf2_salt->data` during the `PKCS5_PBKDF2_HMAC` call, causing a crash.
