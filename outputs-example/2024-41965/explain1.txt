The first free of `buf->b_sfname` occurs at either `VIM_CLEAR(buf->b_fname)` or `VIM_CLEAR(buf->b_ffname)` if these pointers alias `buf->b_sfname`, due to the `buf_set_name()` call setting all three to the same allocated "Untitled" string when the initial filename is NULL. This leads to a double free when `VIM_CLEAR(buf->b_sfname)` is subsequently executed in the same cleanup block after a failed buffer write.
