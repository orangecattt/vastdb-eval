The vulnerability occurs because `NewImageList()` returns `NULL`, but `affinity_image` is not validated after its initial assignment, and `DestroyImage()` includes a `assert(image != (Image *) NULL)` that will cause a crash in debugging builds if the pointer is `NULL`. The execution path shows that `affinity_image` is never reassigned from its initial `NULL` value (as the `CloneImage()` call is conditional and may not execute), leading directly to the dereferencing `assert` in `DestroyImage()`.
